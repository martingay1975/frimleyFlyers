Rules:
  1. Scoring per race:
  2. Scoring per series: Some races have a series of events, e.g. Yateley. Your best time out of all the runs completed in the series will be the scoring one.
  3. If your best effort is a Pb, then you'll be rewarded an additional points. NOTE: The original pb will still be used to base performances.
  4. Scoring overall: Only your best scores are taken through out the season and count towards the final league table. This is allows you to potentially miss races without being penalized.
  5. Scoring is calculated on % difference from persons PB as at start of the year. e.g. if PB is 2 mins, and complete race in 2 mins 12 secs then that is a 10% difference (12 seconds out of 120 seconds).
  6. If a person beats their PB during the year then base PB is not changed. Calculations based on the PB time at start of the year.
  7. Times are taken from the official timers (chip / barcode / web site) and not from personal timings.
  8. If you do not have a best time from the past season for a particular distance, then we shall use the Runners World Race Time Predictor to estimate a time.
  9. You can join at any point during the season. However, any races missed will NOT be retrospectively recorded. An entry fee of £ is required to cover costs.
