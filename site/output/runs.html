Runs

Frimley Lodge parkrun

The nerve centre of the Frimley Flyers. We all started life as parkrunners. Not only do we run but some of us regularly volunteer for all sorts of roles. As a club we also get to do some pacing weeks for those who are feeling brave.

Frimley Lodge parkrun

Tuesday Runs

Various meeting points depending on what the consensus is. Start time 7pm. We normally do some interval training. Some favourite spots are Frimley Lodge Park, Frimley Rec, Farnborough Airport (Templer Avenue), Gun Hill, Deepcut Hill.

Thursday Runs

Various meeting points depending on what the consensus is. Start time 7pm. We normally do either a 5km or 11km starting from Ash Vale train station. Inevitably there are some that trot over to the Swan after. A club favourite, cheesey chips.
